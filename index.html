First, picture the moment that usually triggers this decision.

It’s not always dramatic. Sometimes it’s just the quiet realization that stepping over a tub wall feels less “routine” and more “risky.” Then, the shower becomes shorter, colder, and rushed. Over time, that steals comfort and confidence.

The good news is that a walk in tub can be a real quality-of-life upgrade. However, the wrong tub causes the same frustration in a new way: it does not fit your body, it does not fit your bathroom, or it does not include the features that actually matter to you.

A simple Chicago buying checklist that prevents regret.

Use this like a filter. If a tub fails one step, it is not your tub. Meanwhile, if it passes, you can compare pricing with confidence.

1) Comfort size comes first.

Seat width, door opening, and legroom matter more than a fancy feature list. If you feel cramped, you will not use it the way you hoped.

2) Bathroom fit is non-negotiable.

Chicago bathrooms can be tight, especially in older layouts. Confirm overall length, door swing clearance, and where plumbing ties in. If you want help, call us and we’ll talk through it quickly.

3) Then pick the comfort upgrades.

Fast drain speed, stable grab bar placement, easy controls, and the right seat height are daily wins. After that, consider hydrotherapy if it fits your goal and maintenance preference.

FAQ (Chicago walk in tubs)

What size walk in tub fits most Chicago bathrooms?
Many Chicago-area bathrooms use standard alcove layouts, so 60-inch long tubs are common. However, the correct fit depends on door swing clearance, plumbing location, and your needed seat width.
How do I avoid buying a tub that feels too small?
Start with comfort sizing: seat width, door opening, and legroom. Then confirm your bathroom dimensions. If you are tall or broad shouldered, prioritize interior space over feature lists.
Is one day installation available in the Chicago area?
One day installation may be available depending on layout and site conditions. If you prefer maximum savings, DIY delivery lets your contractor install on your timeline.
What features matter most for daily comfort?
Most buyers value a low step threshold, easy controls, fast draining, stable grab bars, and a seat height that feels natural. After that, hydrotherapy can be considered based on your preferences.
Do you offer hydrotherapy walk in tubs?
