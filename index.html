Chicago Walk In Tub Buying Guide

A Simple Checklist That Prevents Regret

Use this like a filter before you buy anything. If a tub fails one step, it's not your tub.

1

Comfort Sizing Comes First

Seat width, door opening, and interior legroom matter more than any feature list. If you feel cramped in the tub, you won't use it the way you hoped. Tell us your height, weight, and mobility needs \u2014 we'll match you to the right dimensions.

2

Bathroom Fit Is Non-Negotiable

Chicago bathrooms \u2014 especially in older bungalows, Greystone two-flats, and vintage high-rises \u2014 can have tight alcoves and unusual plumbing placements. Confirm your rough opening length, door swing clearance, and drain location before you order. We walk you through this in about 10 minutes on the phone.

3

Daily Usability Features

Low step threshold (under 4 inches), stable grab bars, easy-to-reach controls, fast drain speed, and a comfortable seat height are the features you'll use every single day. These matter more than the add-ons.

4

Then Choose Your Comfort Upgrades

Once fit and usability are confirmed, compare hydrotherapy options: air jets (gentle, bubbly), water jets (targeted massage), or combination systems. Also consider in-line heaters to keep water warm during longer soaks, and self-cleaning jet systems for easier maintenance.
